Borough Park, Brooklyn

Demographics and Statistics

Borough Park contains the zipcodes: 11204, 11218, 11219, 11230
As of 2022, Borough Park had 191,368 residents with 15.7% identifying as Asian, 3.1% as Black, 13.2% as Hispanic and 58.8% as White
The median age in Borough Park is 34.7 years.
The median household income in Borough Park as of 2022 is $68,590 which is 12% lower than the New York City average.
The poverty rate in Borough Park as of 2022 is 23.8%, greater than the citywide average.
